Welcome to Travel Time

Established in 1972, Travel Time is a Destination Management Company operating exclusively for the B2B market across Asia Pacific. Providing personalized travel experiences and MICE programs. We are a leader in our field, being accredited globally with organisations including; the Pacific Asia Travel Association, the Society for Incentive Travel Excellence, and the United States Tour Operators Association.

Headquartered in Singapore, with a secondary hub in Bangkok, we have our own operations and sales offices in Vietnam, Cambodia, Thailand, Myanmar, Malaysia, Indonesia, Philippines, Japan, Korea and Greater China with affiliate offices in Sri Lanka and Laos, as well as sales and marketing representatives in Australia, New Zealand, United States, Europe, Central and Latin America including Mexico and Brazil.

Our drive for constant innovation and quality, is driven by our experienced, passionate and well traveled team. This has earned us a variety of accolades and awards, from national tourism authorities and global travel media, further affirming our position in the market place.
